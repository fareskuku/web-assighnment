About My Country

Ethiopia is very special. It is the only African country that was never colonized. We have our own alphabet called Ge'ez and more than 80 different languages.

Did you know?

  • Capital City: Addis Ababa (which means "New Flower").
  • Main Languages: Amharic, Afaan Oromo, Tigrinya, Somali.
  • Time: Our calendar is 7 years behind the rest of the world!
