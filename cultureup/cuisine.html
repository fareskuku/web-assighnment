Our Cuisine

More than just food, it is a communal experience shared from a single plate.

Staple Food

Injera & Wot

Injera is a sour fermented flatbread with a slightly spongy texture, traditionally made out of Teff flour. It is served with various spicy stews called 'Wot'.

Eating with your hands is a tradition that connects you to the food and the people you are sharing it with.

Drink

The Coffee Ceremony

Ethiopia is the birthplace of coffee (Bunna). The coffee ceremony is an integral part of our social and cultural life.

It involves roasting coffee beans, grinding them in a wooden mortar, and brewing them in a clay pot called a Jebena.
